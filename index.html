How to Sell a Perfume: 5 Things Every Great Advisor Does

Adapted classroom article • Level B1

Selling a perfume is not about pushing a product. It is about listening, understanding, and guiding. The best fragrance advisors use the same five techniques.

01. Ask before you speak
A great advisor starts with a question, not with a bottle.
02. Reformulate
A skilled advisor says the client’s need again in different words to show understanding.
03. Make them smell, not think
Great advisors use sensory language and images, not technical jargon.
04. Handle objections
When the client hesitates, the advisor offers a solution without pressure.
05. Build a relationship
The client should feel welcomed, respected, and understood.
“Great perfume advisors do not sell fragrance. They sell confidence, emotion, and the right choice at the right moment.”
